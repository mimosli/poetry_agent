### 1. Full 15-Second Script

**Text Overlay (Spoken Voice):**  
"In the quiet, we hold our secrets,  
Whispers of what was left unsaid.  
Silent echoes linger in the air,  
Among falling leaves and rippling water,  
Threads of the past, woven into our souls.  
Embrace the silence; it speaks volumes."
